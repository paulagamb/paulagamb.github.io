About

Harry Styles: artist, musician,actor,producer,performer,business man... born on the first of February 1994. His musical carrer began on the year 2010 when he was a participant on the X-factor, leading to him becoming a member of world known band 'One direction'.





He started a solo career in 2017 after the band went on a supposed 'hiatus'and now has 3 albums:'HS1'(self titled) Fine Line and most recently Harry's House, along with touring with these songs around the world and currently touring the US for 'Love On Tour 2022'.


¡Harry Styles has become the only artist to have performed 15 consecutive nights at Madisson Square Garden!





Branching out into other industries he now has a company called 'Pleasing', but his most recent projects are movies, he co-starred along Florence Pugh playing 'jack' in the film 'Don't Worry Darling' and has another one coming out this following month.
